Our Story
BUILT BY ATHLETES,
FOR ATHLETES

IRONCLAD was founded in 2014 by former competitive powerlifter Marcus Drake. After years of training in subpar facilities, he built the gym he always wanted — equipped with the best gear, staffed by the best coaches.

Today, we're home to 2,000+ members ranging from first-timers to professional athletes. Our philosophy is simple: hard work, smart programming, and relentless support.
