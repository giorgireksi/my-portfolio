Our Journey

Crafted with Passion, Served with Elegance

Coral Reef Bistro was born from a dream — to create a dining destination that captures the beauty of the ocean and transforms it into an unforgettable culinary journey.

Founded in 2010 by Executive Chef Marcus Rivera, our restaurant has grown from a small waterfront eatery into one of the city's most celebrated fine dining establishments. Every dish reflects our commitment to quality, creativity, and the art of hospitality.

We source ingredients from local farms and sustainable fisheries, ensuring that every plate tells a story of freshness and ethical sourcing. Our team of master chefs brings decades of combined experience from Michelin-starred kitchens around the world.

— Chef Marcus Rivera
