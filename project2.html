Our Story

Built on Integrity,
Driven by Results

Sterling & Associates was founded in 1998 by Jonathan Sterling with a simple mission: provide elite legal representation to those who need it most. From a small office in downtown Manhattan, we've grown into a nationally recognized firm with a reputation for winning.

Our team of 30+ attorneys brings collective expertise from the nation's top law schools and firms. We've represented Fortune 500 companies, government entities, and individuals in some of the most significant cases in recent history.

What sets us apart isn't just our track record — it's our approach. Every case receives the same level of dedication, strategic thinking, and aggressive advocacy, regardless of size.
