Our Story
BUILT ON TRUST,
DRIVEN BY EXCELLENCE

Founded in 2004 by master technician Ray Kowalski, APEX AUTO has grown from a 2-bay neighborhood shop into Brooklyn's most trusted full-service repair center — without ever losing the personal touch.

Every technician on our team is ASE-certified with a minimum of 8 years hands-on experience. We work on all makes and models — from daily drivers to high-performance vehicles — with OEM and premium aftermarket parts.
